15 Year Party Supplies is an official add-on developed by Oreville Studios released to celebrate the 15th anniversary of Minecraft.

Additions Edit

Items Edit

  • 15 Year Balloon
    • Spawns balloons in a "15" shape attached to the block below when used
  • 15 Year Candle
    • Used to craft 15 year cakes
    • Crafted using one string, one honeycomb, and one gold ingot
  • 15 Year Party Supplies Handbook
    • Guides player on how to craft a 15 year cake, and the various items party villagers give
  • Black Party Horn
    • Shows an animation of the party horn being blown and extending when used
  • Blue Balloon
    • Spawns a blue balloon attached to the block below when used
  • Blue Party Horn
    • Shows an animation of the party horn being blown and extending when used
  • Bubble Machine
    • Spawns a bubble machine when used on a block
    • Continuously blows bubble particles in one direction
    • Destroyed when damaged
  • Bubble Wand
    • Blows bubbles in front of the player when used
    • Has a short cooldown
  • Candy
    • Dropped from pinata creepers
    • Can be eaten to restore 1 () and grant Jump Boost for a short duration
  • Confetti Cannon
  • Confetti Machine
  • Creeper Balloon
    • Spawns a creeper-themed balloon attached to the block below when used
  • Cupcake
    • Grants Speed for two seconds
  • Party Villager Spawn Egg
    • Spawns a party villager when used
  • Pinata Creeper
    • Spawns a pinata creeper when used
  • Red Balloon
    • Spawns a red balloon attached to the block below when used
  • Red Party Horn
    • Shows an animation of the party horn being blown and extending when used
  • Yellow Balloon
    • Spawns a yellow balloon attached to the block below when used
  • Yellow Party Horn
    • Shows an animation of the party horn being blown and extending when used

Blocks Edit

  • 15 Year Banner
    • A banner with the 15th anniversary logo design
    • Cannot be placed on the sides of blocks
    • Doesn't need a block for support
  • 15 Year Cake
    • Crafted using one 15 Year Candle and one cake
    • Summons a party villager when lit
  • Bunting Green
  • Bunting White
  • Cake Slice
  • Confetti
  • Giant Cake Center
  • Giant Cake Corner
  • Giant Cake Edge
  • Green Present
  • Pile of Presents
  • Purple Present
  • Sparkler
  • Red Present

Mobs Edit

  • Party Villager
    • Spawns when a 15 year cake is lit using flint and steal, consuming the cake upon being spawned
    • Gives individual anniversary items, similar to trading but without any currency
    • Can be despawned on command in the item UI
