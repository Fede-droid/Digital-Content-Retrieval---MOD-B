4J Studios which stands for Jute, Jam, Journalism (the 3J's of Dundee), and Joysticks,[1] is an independent game development studio based in Scotland, with offices in Dundee and East Linton. The staff includes many former key personnel from defunct studio VIS Entertainment. It was founded by Paddy Burns and Chris van der Kuyl.[2] They are best known for developing the Legacy Console Edition of Minecraft.

Games Edit

4J Studios has developed the following games:

  • Minecraft: Xbox 360 Edition (Xbox 360) (2012)
  • Minecraft: PlayStation 3 Edition (PS3) (2013)
  • Minecraft: PlayStation 4 Edition (PS4) (2014)
  • Minecraft: Xbox One Edition (Xbox One) (2014)
  • Minecraft: PlayStation Vita Edition (PS Vita, PS TV) (2014)
  • Minecraft: Wii U Edition (Wii U) (2015)
  • Minecraft: Nintendo Switch Edition (Nintendo Switch) (2017)

They have ported/remade the following games to other consoles:

  • The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion (PS3) (2007)
  • Overlord: Raising Hell (PS3) (2008)
  • Banjo-Kazooie (Xbox 360) (2008)
  • Banjo-Tooie (Xbox 360) (2009)

4J Studios has developed the following original games:

  • Breeders' Cup World Thoroughbred Championships (Xbox, PS2) (2005)
  • Star Trek: Encounters (PS2) (2006)
  • Star Trek: Conquest (Wii, PS2) (2007)
  • AMF Bowling Pinbusters! (Nintendo DS) (2008)
  • Ducati Moto (Nintendo DS) (2008)
  • Wuggle (iOS) (2009)
  • Texas Wuggle (iOS) (2009)
  • Perfect Dark (Xbox 360) (2010)
  • Manic Mechanics (Nintendo Switch) (2023) (Windows, Xbox One, PS4) (2024)

4J Studios has published the following original games:

  • Skye Tales (Windows, Nintendo Switch, PS4) (2023)
