Blackbird Interactive is a Canadian video game development studio based in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. The company is responsible for developing Minecraft Legends alongside Mojang Studios, and was also previously responsible in assisting Mojang with development of Minecraft Earth.

Games developed Edit

Blackbird Interactive has developed and published one original game, Project Eagle, in collaboration with NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory, as an interactive art demo of a human colony on Mars. It was released on November 27, 2018, for PC.[1]

The company has also developed the following games:[1]

  • Homeworld: Deserts of Kharak (PC/OSX, 2016)
  • Minecraft Earth (Android/iOS/iPadOS, 2019)
  • Hardspace: Shipbreaker (PC/PS4/ONE, 2022)
  • Crossfire: Legion (PC, 2022)
  • Minecraft Legends (PC/PlayStation/Xbox/NSwitch, 2023)
  • Homeworld 3 (PC, 2024)
  • Earthless (PC, 2024)
