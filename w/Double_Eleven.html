Double Eleven is a British game development studio based in Middlesbrough, England.

The studio is responsible for developing Minecraft Dungeons alongside Mojang Studios and Xbox Game Studios, particularly with multiplayer, DLC content and the console versions.[1]

Games Edit

Double Eleven has developed / published the following games:[2]

  • PixelJunk Eden (PS3/Windows, 2009-2012)
  • Call of Duty: Black Ops – Zombies (iOS, 2011)
  • Lego Batman 2: DC Super Heroes (iOS, 2012)
  • LittleBigPlanet PS Vita (Vita, 2012)
  • Lego Star Wars: The Complete Saga (iOS, 2013)
  • Lego Marvel Super Heroes: Universe in Peril (iOS, 2013)
  • PixelJunk Shooter (Windows/Mac/Linux, 2013)
  • Limbo (PS4/Vita/ONE, 2013-2015)
  • PixelJunk Monsters (Vita/Windows/Mac/Linux/WiiU, 2013-2016)
  • Frozen Synapse Prime (Windows/Mac/Linux/iOS/Android/PS3/Vita, 2014)
  • Goat Simulator (PS3/PS4/360/ONE/Win10, 2015)
  • Nom Nom Galaxy (PS4, 2015)
  • Inside (Windows/ONE/PS4, 2016)
  • Lego Harry Potter Collection (PS4, 2016)
  • Mantis Burn Racing (Windows/ONE/PS4/NSwitch, 2016-2017)
  • Prison Architect (360/ONE/PS4/NSwitch, 2016-2018)
  • Songbringer (ONE/PS4/Windows, 2017)
  • Super Cloudbuilt (ONE/PS4/Windows, 2017)
  • Doctor Who Infinity (iOS/Android/Fire, 2018)
  • Fallout 76 (ONE/PS4/Windows, 2018)
  • Crackdown 3 (ONE/Win10, 2019)
  • This Is Pool (PS4/ONE/Windows/NSwitch, 2019)
  • Minecraft Dungeons (ONE/PS4/NSwitch, 2020)
  • Rust (ONE/PS4, 2020)
  • Red Dead Redemption (NSwitch/PS4, 2023)
