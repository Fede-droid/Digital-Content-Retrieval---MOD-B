"Ancient" redirects here. For the structure that generates in the deep dark, see Ancient City. For the resource used to create netherite found in the nether, see Ancient Debris.

An ancient is a boss-like variant of a common mob that have various enchantments along with enchanted minions and drop special gilded items. Each ancient appears in a unique ancient arena that the hero may encounter when entering hunt doors during an Ancient Hunt.[1].

The ancients that appear during a given Ancient Hunt are selected at the start of a hunt by sacrificing a set of ancient offerings. Each ancient has a specific rune combination that are required for their ancient arena to appear in an Ancient Hunt. Only one melee weapon, one ranged weapon, one armor, and/or one artifact may be offered at a time. Multiple ancients can be called by a if the runes combinations to summon them are fufilled. The chance of hunt doors leading to ancient arenas instead of gold doors is increased with the number of enchantment points invested.
