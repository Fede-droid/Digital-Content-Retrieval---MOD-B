Earth:Mob

From Minecraft Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search
Minecraft Earth was shut down due to outdoor restrictions as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic. 
It can no longer be downloaded or played, but the contents of this page are kept for historical interest.
For mobs in Minecraft, see Mob.
For mob that looks like the player, see Minecraft Earth:Mob of Me.

A mob, short for mobile entity, was an AI-driven entity. There were several mobs exclusively found in Minecraft Earth that are not present in Minecraft, the original game.
