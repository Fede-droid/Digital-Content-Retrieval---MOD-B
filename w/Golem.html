The term Golem may refer to several golem or golem-like mobs:

Minecraft[edit | edit source]

  • Iron Golem - a neutral mob naturally spawned in villages (passive if made by the player)
    • Iron Golem Spawn Egg
  • Love Golem - a version of the iron golem for April Fools' Day 2015
  • Snow Golem - a passive mob created from snow blocks and a carved pumpkin or jack o'lantern
    • Snow Golem Spawn Egg

Minecraft Education[edit | edit source]

  • Agent - a passive mob exclusive to Minecraft Education
    • Agent Spawn Egg

Minecraft Live[edit | edit source]

  • Copper Golem - a mob in the Minecraft Live 2021 mob vote that would have operated copper buttons.
  • Tuff Golem - a mob in the Minecraft Live 2022 mob vote that would have displayed items.

Minecraft: Story Mode[edit | edit source]

  • Iron Golem
  • Icy Golem - a hostile mob created by Romeo that are a cross of iron and snow golems
  • Larger Icy Golem - a large boss variant of the Icy Golems and one of the challenges of Romeo
  • Prison Golem - a neutral mob created by Romeo and used to keep prisioners on their places
  • Magma Golem - a hostile mob created by Romeo and used as challenges for himself, Xara and Fred
  • Giant Magma Golem - a gigantic boss created by Romeo as the final challenge for himself, Xara, and Fred
  • Prismarine Soldier - a hostile prismarine mob founded in ocean monument
  • Prismarine Creeper - a scrapped prismarine variant of creeper
  • Prismarine Colossus - a gigantic version of Prismarine Soldier

Minecraft Earth[edit | edit source]

  • Furnace Golem - an iron golem with a blast furnace body
  • Melon Golem - a snow golem with a melon head

Minecraft Dungeons[edit | edit source]

Main article: MCD:Golem
  • Iron Golem - a mob summoned using the Golem Kit artifact
  • Key Golem - a mob used to open doors
  • Mooshroom Monstrosity - the revived remains of the Redstone Monstrosity
  • Obsidian Monstrosity - an obsidian version of the Redstone Monstrosity
  • Redstone Cube - a mob that is summoned and was originally part of the Redstone Monstrosity
  • Redstone Golem - a powerful mob found in the Redstone Mines
  • Unbreakable One - ancient version of Redstone Golem
  • Redstone Monstrosity - the boss of the Fiery Forge mission
  • Stone Ministrosity - a pet found in the adventure hub.
    • Redstone Ministrosity - a pet found in the adventure hub.
    • Glowing Ministrosity - a pet found in the adventure hub.
    • Mini Abomination - a pet mini version of Jungle Abomination found in the Adventure Hub.
    • Glowing Mini Abomination - a glowing version of Mini Abomination found in the Adventure Hub
  • Squall Golem - a hostile mob from the Howling Peaks DLC
  • Tempest Golem - the final boss of the Howling Peaks DLC
  • Treetop Whisperer - the boss of the Treetop Tangle mission.[1]
  • Iron Golem Cape
  • Diamond Key Golem - diamond version of Key Golem
  • Adventure Hub
  • Jungle Abomination - large wooden boss created by orb of domination

Minecraft Legends[edit | edit source]

Main article: MCL:Golem
  • Action - one of the host in Minecraft Legends
  • Cobblestone Golem - a golem created by flames of creation
  • Grindstone Golem - a golem created by hosts
  • Mossy Golem - a golem created by hosts
  • Plank Golem - a golem created flames of creation
  • First of Brick - an ancient golem that helped its owners feed the overworld
  • First of Diorite - an ancient golem that helped its owners feed the overworld
  • First of Oak - an ancient golem that helped its owners feed the overworld
  • First of Stone - an ancient golem that helped its owners feed the overworld
