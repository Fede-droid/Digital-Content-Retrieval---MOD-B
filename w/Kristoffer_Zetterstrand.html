Kristoffer Zetterstrand is a Swedish artist. Kristoffer primarily paints classical paintings, but they are often influenced by pixelated computer graphics and 3D worlds. Most of the paintings in Minecraft were created by him. The file in which the paintings are stored is named paintings_kristoffer_zetterstrand.png in Java Edition prior to 1.14 and kz.png for his initials in Bedrock Edition.

Kristoffer is Ez's brother, and is Notch's former brother-in-law.[1]
