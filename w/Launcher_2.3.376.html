2.3.376 was an update to the launcher released on August 19, 2022.

This update especially includes the re-addition of Korean language back to the launcher, as it was deleted from the launcher three weeks prior to this update due to the low approval percentage in Crowdin.

Additionally, this update also introduced new appearances for the default Steve & Alex skins.[1]
