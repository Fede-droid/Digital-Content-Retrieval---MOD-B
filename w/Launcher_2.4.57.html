2.4.57 was an update to the launcher released on May 5, 2023.

Contents

  • 1 Changes
  • 2 Fixes
  • 3 Navigation

Changes[edit | edit source]

  • Various availability updates.
  • Improved file distribution.

Fixes[edit | edit source]

  • Fixed a bug related to launcher button animation.
  • Fixed a bug related to account mismatch warnings.
  • MCL-23150 - One of the system requirement strings is used across multiple pages in the Minecraft Launcher limiting its translatability.
  • MCL-23322 - Minecraft Legends "Play" button glitches out during Piglin animation.
