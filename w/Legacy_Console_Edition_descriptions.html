This edition has been discontinued.

In Legacy Console Edition, blocks, items and entities all had dedicated descriptions, which would appear on-screen in a blue dialog box of sorts when the subject was first encountered, or when "What’s This?" was clicked on it in the inventory.

List Edit

Blocks and items are listed according to their order in the Legacy Console Edition Creative inventory, including some additional obtainable block and item variants not found in the Creative inventory on Legacy Console. Other technical blocks are listed separately. All tables are sortable and can be sorted alphabetically.
