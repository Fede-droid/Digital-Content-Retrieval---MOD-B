Lost Legends are monthly free challenges. They can be downloaded free of purchase through the marketplace. Completing the required challenge in a lost legend will reward either a mount or hero skin.

List of Lost Legends[edit | edit source]

  • The Portal Pile
    • The portal pile lost legend is where you defend from 20 hordes of piglins coming from indestructible nether portals.
  • The Breakout
    • The second lost legend is all about rescuing your friends while fighting the Unbreakable.
  • Legend of the Llama
    • The third lost legend is all about leading llamas against 4 nether portals while also playing as a llama.
  • The Hunting Season
    • The fourth lost legend is about fighting the Beast while defending 5 villages.
  • The Crucible
    • The fifth lost legend is about fighting through nine chambers of overwhelming piglin danger.
  • Treetop Trampoline... of DOOM!
    • The sixth lost legend is about bouncing around in the jungle with bouncecaps while attacking Horde of the Spore bases.
  • Creeper Clash
    • The seventh lost legend is about destroying the piglin bases and piglin outposts while only using creepers.
  • When Pigs Fly
    • The eighth lost legend is about battling 10 waves of barrel-flung piglins in a colosseum.
  • Snow vs Snouts
    • The ninth lost legend is about redstone launchers that shoot snow blocks instead of TNT. This is confirmed to be the last ever lost legend. [1]

Rewards[edit | edit source]

  • The Portal Buster Hero skin rewarded to players that completed the Portal Pile lost legend.

  • The Prison Break Big Beak skin rewarded to players that completed the Breakout lost legend.

  • The Honorary Camelid Horse skin rewarded to players that completed the Legend of the Llama lost legend.

  • The Piglin Pursuer Hero skin rewarded to players that completed the Hunting Season lost legend.

  • The Crucible Champion Hero skin rewarded to players that completed the Crucible lost legend.

  • The Obsidian Beetle Brilliant Beetle skin rewarded to players that completed the Treetop Trampoline... of DOOM! lost legend.

  • The Spooky Swathe Hero skin rewarded to players that completed the Creeper Clash lost legend.

  • The Emerald Cheetah Regal Tiger skin rewarded to players that completed the When Pigs Fly lost legend.

  • The Snow Guardian Hero skin rewarded to players that completed the Snow vs Snouts lost legend.
