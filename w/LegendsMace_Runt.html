A mace runt is a piglin runt that wields a mace within Minecraft Legends.

Spawning Edit

Mace runts can be found in all hordes - the Horde of the Hunt, horde of the Spore, and horde of the Bastion. Also found in groups. Will sometimes be summoned by:

Piglin Pit

Piglin Launcher

Mace runts are common in the Overworld at night.

Drops Edit

Mace runts drop lapis.

Behavior Edit

Mace Melee

  • Mace runts use maces to attack their enemies.

"Mace runts are the weakest piglins, but they make up for this with their numbers and their ferocious determination to rise up the ranks. Their maces are only dangerous in close combat, and they are susceptible to long range attacks. They're pretty cute when they're not pillaging or setting things on fire. Unfortunately, most of the time they're pillaging and setting things on fire."
