Structures are buildings that are constructed by either the player or piglins in Minecraft Legends.

Structure types[edit | edit source]

Buildings[edit | edit source]

A building is a type of structure buildable by the player that provides some form of defensive benefit.

  • Extendable Wall
  • Ramp
  • Gate
  • Cauldron
  • Ice Trap
  • Scatter Tower
  • Wellhouse
  • Spyglass Overlook
  • Redstone Launcher
  • Stun Tower
  • Battle Drum
  • Protector Tower
  • Kaboomery
  • Frost Tower
  • Blast Tower
  • Carpenter Hut
  • Arrow Tower
  • Lapis Flag
  • Masonry

Improvements[edit | edit source]

Main article: Minecraft Legends:Improvement

An improvement is a type of structure that can only be built in a small radius around improvement hubs. When built, a benefit dependent on the improvement built is granted to the player and their allies.

  • Improvement Hub
  • Gather Coal
  • Gather Redstone
  • Gather Iron
  • Gather Diamond
  • Build/Wake the Firsts
  • Abundant Allays
  • Banner
  • Allay Storage
  • Flames of Creation
  • Build/Collect Power Towers

Ally settlements[edit | edit source]

  • Village
  • Witch Hut
  • Well of Fate
  • Homestead
    • Zombie Homestead
    • Skeleton Homestead
    • Creeper Homestead
    • Spider Homestead (unused)

Piglin structures[edit | edit source]

Piglin structures are a type of structure that spawn in piglin outposts and bases and work against the player.

  • Piglin Wall
  • Piglin Cage
  • Piglin Gate
  • Gold Extractor
  • Nether Portal
  • Lava Coil
  • Blaze Rod Tower
  • Armored Tower
  • Air Chopper
  • Nether Spreader
  • Spore Tower
  • Terror Horn
  • Spike Tower
  • Piglin Pit
  • Patcher
  • Piglin Launcher
  • Siegeworks
