Minecraft typically refers to the game in general. It may also refer to:

Editions Edit

Main article: Edition
  • Java Edition: the original edition for Windows, macOS, and Linux.
  • Bedrock Edition: the edition for Windows, ChromeOS, Android, iOS, iPadOS, Fire, Xbox One, Xbox Series X|S, Nintendo Switch, PS4, and PS VR, under the name Minecraft.
  • Minecraft Education: the edition for classrooms.
  • China Edition: the edition for mainland China.
  • Minecraft 4k: the under 4 kibibytes version of the game made for the 2010 Java 4K contest.
  • MinecraftEdu: the discontinued edition for classrooms.
  • Legacy Console Edition: the discontinued edition for various consoles (Xbox 360, Xbox One (legacy version), PS3, PS4 (legacy version), PS Vita, PS TV, Wii U, and Nintendo Switch (legacy version)).
  • Pi Edition: the discontinued edition for the Raspberry Pi.
  • New Nintendo 3DS Edition: the discontinued edition for the New Nintendo 3DS/2DS systems.

Other games Edit

  • Demo: the Minecraft demo versions.
  • Minecraft: Story Mode: the narrative-driven video game.
    • Minecraft: Story Mode - Season Two: the sequel to Minecraft: Story Mode.
  • Minecraft Earth: a discontinued augmented reality (AR) mobile game spin-off.
  • Minecraft Dungeons: an action-adventure role-playing game spin-off.
    • Minecraft Dungeons Arcade: an arcade adaptation of Minecraft Dungeons.
  • Minecraft Legends: an real-time action strategy game spin-off.

Miscellaneous Edit

  • Minecraft (franchise): the Minecraft franchise.
  • Minecraft Launcher: the game downloader and launcher for Java Edition and one of the game downloaders and launchers for Bedrock Edition and Minecraft Dungeons.
    • Minecraft Dungeons Launcher: was one of the game downloaders and launchers for Minecraft Dungeons.
  • .minecraft: the game data folder.
  • Minecraft (song): the soundtrack name for one of the music tracks in the game.
  • Minecraft 2.0: a joke update made for April Fools 2013.
  • Minecraft: The Movie: an upcoming Swedish–American film due for an unknown release.
  • Minecraft: an advancement by getting a crafting table in your inventory.
  • Minecraft Magazine: the official magazine of Minecraft.
  • Minecraft (Dark Horse Comics): a graphic novel series based on the video game.
  • Minecraft Hour of Code: a tutorial designed to introduce the player to programming logic.
  • Minecraft Books: a series of guidebooks for Minecraft.
  • Minecraft Plus!: a screensaver app and website for an April Fools 2021 joke.
  • Minecraft (Step into Reading series): a children's book series based on the video game.
  • Minecraft: Builders & Biomes: a board game published by Mojang Studios and Ravensburger.
  • Minecraft Card Game: a card game published by Mojang Studios and Mattel Games.
