Minecraft: Creeper Crunch was a breakfast cereal that was produced by Kellogg's in the United States, consisting of pulverized oats flavored with cinnamon and "Creeper Bit" marshmallows molded into cubes.[2] The box also contained a code which was able to be redeemed for a rare character creator item, in which sixteen were available through this method.

Nutrition information Edit

All nutrition information is available publicly from SmartLabel and is based off of a 2,000-calorie diet.

Trivia Edit

  • Originally, only ten character creation items were available through this method, three more items were later added and finally three last items to come to a total of 16 items.
  • Originally, this cereal was planned to be discontinued on August 31, 2021, but was pushed back to June 30, 2022 for unknown reasons.

Issues Edit

The Kellogg's™ - Minecraft In Game Content Offer Terms and Conditions have not been updated on the number of items available by the codes. This mention does not prevent the legality of the terms and conditions but can create confusion, despite the sale of the boxes and the large number of sources on the internet proving very different versions of the creeper crunch as well as the number of items actually available.
