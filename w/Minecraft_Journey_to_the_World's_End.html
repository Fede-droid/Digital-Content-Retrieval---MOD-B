Minecraft: Journey to the World's End (MINECRAFT〜世界の果てへの旅〜, Minecraft: Sekai no Hate e no Tabi) is a Japanese manga series published by Shogakukan and serialized in the monthly CoroCoro Comic magazine.

The manga series is popular in Japan, with a recorded 600,000 sales for the first 8 volumes.[2]

Plot Edit

Niko White is a member of the White Family, a family of adventurers with the ability to access the Mod Ability Creativity. After being overrun by Zombies while mining, Niko awakens the ability himself, using it to craft a Super Long Stone Sword. He decides to fulfill his dream of reaching the World's End, like his father left to do himself, and sets out on a journey.

Characters Edit

  • Niko White (ニコ・ホワイト, Niko Howaito): A young boy from the White Family (ホワイト家, Howaito-ka), and the protagonist. He is 10 years old and was born in Teo Village (テオ村, Teo-mura).
  • Gray (グレイ, Gurei): A man cursed by a Mod Ability that transformed him into a Zombie. He travels the world trying to find a cure to his ability, joining Niko on his journey. Unlike the rest of his village, he kept his intelligence even after being transformed
  • Yamabuki (ヤマブキ, Yamabuki): A ninja who aims to the strongest swordsman. He was kicked out of his village and has no talent. He joins Niko on his journey.
  • Elma White (エルマ・ホワイト, Eruma Howaito): Niko's mother and Ikko's wife.
  • Ikko White (イッコ・ホワイト, Ikko Howaito), Niko's father and Elma's husband.
  • Luke (ルーク, Rūku) and Bull (ブル, Buru): Members of the Brown Family (ブラウン家, Buraun-ka), they call themselves the Brown Brothers (ブラウンブラザーズ, Buraun Burazāzu). They are Niko's self-proclaimed rivals.
  • Navy (ネイビー, Neibī): A small Creeper trying to reach the World's End. He can speak, and joins Niko on his journey.
  • Lily (リリィ, Riryi): The daughter of a village chief. Niko helped her deal with monsters that attacked animals.
  • Dylan (ディラン, Diran): A sailor that Niko met at a port village.
  • Phoenix (フェニックス, Fenikkusu): The leader of Magma Village (マグマの村, Maguma no Mura).
  • Fox (フォックス, Fokkusu): A traveling peddler.
  • Platinum (プラチナ, Purachina): An Iron Golem designed to look like a human woman. She joins Niko on his journey.
  • Rock (ロック, Rokku): An adventurer with information on the Ender Dragon. He claims that he once traveled with Ikko White.
  • Don Gold Flavor (ドン・ゴールドー味, Don Gōrudō-mi): A bandit trio consisting of Don Gold (ドン・ゴールド, Don Gōrudō), Ruby (ルビー, Rubī), and Hunt (ハント, Hanto). They chased Niko's party out of Metis Village (メティス村, Metisu-mura) in an attempt to get his Mod Ability.
  • Oulu (オウル, Ōru): A man who travels with Ikko White. He has the Clairvoyance Mod Ability, located in his left eye. Due to this, he usually covers both his eyes with cloth.
  • Sapphire (サファイア, Safaia): A crafter Niko's party met, who already knew of Niko. She carries a giant pickaxe.

Mod Ability Edit

Mod Abilities (MOD能力, Moddo Nōryoku), also called Cursed Mods by Gray, are intrinsic special abilities. They appear to be passed down from family members. Three Mod Abilities are known:

  • Creativity (創造力, Sōzō-ryoku), used by the White Family (Niko White and Ikko White).
    • Creativity allows the user to craft in any sized grid, even without the use of a Crafting Table. In addition to normal items (such as a Wooden Sword), it can be used to craft items invented by the user. So far, two such items have been shown:
      • Super Long Stone Sword (超ロング石の剣, Chō Rongu Ishi no Ken), crafted using a Stone Sword recipe with four cobblestone instead of two.
      • Wooden Fuma Shuriken, crafted by placing four Wooden Swords above, below, and beside an empty space.
  • An unnamed Zombie Mod Ability, which Gray and his village are afflicted by.
  • Clairvoyance (千里眼, Senrigan), used by Oulu.

Those affected by Mod Abilities have Mod Crests on their hand, which differ for each ability.
