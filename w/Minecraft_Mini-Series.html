The Minecraft Mini-Series is a Canadian-American action/adventure animated web series based on Mojang Studios's Minecraft. The series is produced by Mojang Studios and Xbox Game Studios in collaboration with Atomic Cartoons and Mattel Creations, with Christopher Keenan serving as executive producer, and premiered on October 26, 2017, on the Mattel Action YouTube channel, with the final episode being released on May 10, 2018. It was met with mixed reviews.

Voice cast Edit

  • Lydia Winters as CommuniKate, the host of the show.
  • Kyle Rideout as Durango, part of Team Mesa.
  • Rebecca Shoichet as Quinn, part of Team Stone Beach.
  • Sarah Edmondson as River, part of Team Mesa.
  • Omari Newton as Rudi and Zeus.
  • Diana Kaarina as Sacha, part of Team Tundra.
  • Cathy Weseluck as Slugger, part of Team Mooshroom.
  • Bill Newton as Toby, part of Team Stone Beach.
  • Sabina B. as Mo, part of Team Mooshroom.
  • Ian Hanlin as Poseidon, Theseus and Chimera, all part of Greek mythology.
