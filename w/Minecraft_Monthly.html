“

Minecraft Monthly is a new series where we bring you Minecraft news, sneak peeks, and the occasional unsolicited fashion tip.

„
— Minecraft YouTube Channel[1]

Minecraft Monthly is a series of normally monthly news videos aimed at giving a comedic rundown of recent and upcoming features regarding the Minecraft franchise.[1]

List of episodes Edit

Airing Host Notes
March 2, 2023[1]
Vu Bui
 - Revealed the name of the 1.20 Update (Trails & Tales).

- Recapped the latest Minecraft 1.20 features.

- Revealed and showcased the Minecraft Crocs.

- Announced more information regarding Minecraft Legends.

March 22, 2023[2] - Showcased a sniffer egg hatching in-game.

- Highlighted a 10-year-old bug regarding horse breeding which was fixed.

- Showcased a new updated emotes UI on Bedrock Edition and emote appears on the chat.

- Announced the Artemis: Rocket Build DLC for Bedrock and Education Edition as they collaborated with NASA.

April 28, 2023[3] - Showcased Minecraft Legends and its gameplay, as well as the monthly Lost Legends challenges.

- Teased their new Youtube series - Minecraft: The Great Wild.

- Also talked about Trails & Tales update news and Bedrock Edition Marketplace maps.

- Announced 5 new soundtracks coming to the Trails & Tales Update and the relic music disc in-game.

- Showcased craftable banners on shield for Bedrock Edition.

- Announced the SpongeBob SquarePants DLC for Bedrock Edition.

- Showcased rhe Nature Snap for Bedrock Edition as a way to celebrate Earth Day.

May 26, 2023[4] - Revealed the release date of Trails & Tales Update.

- Showcased all new features for the Trails & Tales Update.

- Announced a new monthly update for Minecraft Legends - Lost Legends

June 21, 2023[5] - Showcased all new features for the Trails & Tales Update again.

- Mini-game: Trails & Tales Blockbuster Battle with 4 developers as a way to celebrate the release of Trails & Tales Update.

- Showcased sneaking improvements and crawling for Bedrock Edition.

- Showcased Agnes with a real-life camel. - Showcased Minecraft for Google Chromebook.

- Announced a new update for Minecraft Legends - Lost Legends: The Legends of the Llama.

- Announced the Summer Event as a live server event available on Bedrock Edition.

September 22, 2023[6] - Revealed the new Minecraft Monthly logo based on other Minecraft events such as Minecraft Live or Minecraft Now.

- Announced more information regarding Minecraft Live 2023.

- Showcased the new expeimental feature which changed and updated the Villager trading system.

- Announced the Minecraft Dungeons soundtracks coming to vynl and Lo-Fi Minecraft Melody series on YouTube.

- Announced the Dungeons & Dragons DLC for Bedrock Edition.

- Announced a new update for Minecraft Legends - Lost Legends: The Treetop Trampoline of Doom

- Talked about ducks as a way to celebrate the views of a short video on TikTok.

November 8, 2023[7] - Showcased features for Update 1.21 that were revealed during Minecraft Live 2023.

- Revealed some early concept arts for the armadillo mob.

- Announced the Star Wars - Patch of the Jedi DLC for Bedrock Edition.

- Announced Minecraft Realms for Snapshots/Betas and Previews.

- Announced a new update for Minecraft Legends - Lost Legends: When Pigs fly

December 12, 2023[8] - Showcased the new armadillo mob and the new wolf armor in-game.

- Announced the Jurassic World Adventure DLC for Bedrock Edition and New Year's Celebration.

- Showcased features for Java Edition 1.20.3 and Bedrock Edition 1.20.50 Updates.

- Announced a new update for Minecraft Legends - Lost Legends and revealed the new piglin mob - Clanger.

January 26, 2024[9] - Showcased updated armadillo design with a brighter color and new eyes placement.

- Announced the Planet Earth II for Education Edition.

- Community Creations: World's longest Minecraft staircase.

- Announced the vault and the usage of trial keys.

March 6, 2024[10] - Showcased the wind charge, the bogged, dyable and stronger wolf armor.

- Showcased and revealed 8 new wolf variants.

- Showcased hardcore mode and Marketplace Add-ons for Bedrock Edition.

April 26, 2024[11] - Showcased the Armored Paws Drop.

- Recapped the latest Minecraft 1.21 features, briefly mentioning 3, at the time, not-yet-announced Music Discs.

- Showcased Minecraft 1.21's new paintings and soundtrack.

- Revealed the name of the 1.21 Update (Tricky Trials).
