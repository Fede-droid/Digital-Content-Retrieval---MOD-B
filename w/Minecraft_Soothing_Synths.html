Minecraft: Soothing Synths is an extended play released for Minecraft, featuring compositions by Aaron Cherof and remixed by Monolism. It consists of the songs that were released as the soundtrack for Trails & Tales, but remixed in a "lo-fi" style. It was released on September 18, 2023.[1] Alongside the Minecraft Dungeons and Minecraft Legends remixes, one track from the EP is featured in the Minecraft Launcher and can be toggled with the play button in the top-right corner.
