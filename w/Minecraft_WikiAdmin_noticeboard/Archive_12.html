Update: It seems to be a caching problem on Curse's side. The feed is not giving me new changes (such as the message I wrote just above). However, if I change the URL, such as by adding a bogus parameter &thismeansnothingtomediawiki=1, then I get new content — and if I change it back I see the old content.
