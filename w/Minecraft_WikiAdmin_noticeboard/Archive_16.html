Editcount

I see that many people here are using Kanegasi's editcounter script. I was just wondering if the MediaWiki Editcount extension has been considered as an alternative? It runs from Special Pages and doesn't require the user to configure anything in their own Javascript page. This is not to disrespect the wonderful work Kanegasi has done, it is just a reflection of the fact that many users may not be capable of, nor have the inclination to, edit their own *.js files. To see the MediaWiki Editcount extension in action, have a look at the UESP Wiki's implementation of it. Feel free to look me up there.
