Filter catching edit incorrectly

I tried to post this on Chest's talk page earlier, but an automated filter thinks that there are external links in it and won't let me post. Help?

== One-way doors with chests instead of Soul Sand ==

I trust you all know about the one-way doorways, possible with Soul Sand. The bug is based off of the idea that Soul Sand is seven-eighths the height of normal blocks. Well, so are chests! You can do one-way doorways with chests! Chests are MUCH cheeper. And this also means you don't need pistons or anything for trapping mobs. Here's a good mob trap. Replace a ground block with a chest, and put a temporary block three blocks higher (leaving a two-high gap). Place four blocks around the temporary block, and remove the temporary block. Mobs can't escape it! (Except for spiders.) So, MUCH CHEEPER One-way doors and mob traps.

As for the "we can jump over the chests" objection for one-way doors, just do what is done with Soul Sand, such as making an area of chests around the doorway instead of just one, since you need to jump from a full block to get through. (If you can't place down a chest, because it's near a double chest, use a trapped chest instead.)
If you can't make trapped chests, then here's another trick. Raise the door and chest up one, and place a temporary block above the chest. Put a block to the right and to the left of the temporary block, and then place a block on each of those. Now remove the temporary block. Then make the chest a double chest, pointing away from the door. As far as I can tell, this always works.

If you put two of these mob traps (the type that spiders can escape) near each other, by the way, then you can get a pretty cheap record farm. Lure a creeper into one trap, a skelly into the other, and let the skelly shoot the creeper.

This is useful for people who are either very scared of the Nether, don't have a Nether portal and are impatient, don't want to waste a non-renewable block, are playing on servers without the Nether, or just don't like Soul Sand.
