Template:ID table outputs wrong names

It seems ID table just does a simple transformation on the item names, so it gives minecraft:wood_planks for "Wood Planks" and minecraft:magma_block for "Magma Block", for example, but those are not the correct names, they are minecraft:planks and minecraft:magma, respectively. The template needs to be changed to show the proper names instead of making such an assumption on the format of the names. nyuszika7h (talk) 16:05, 27 June 2016 (UTC)

There's a nameid parameter which does that:
{{Data table|Wood Planks|nameid=planks}}
{{Data table|Magma Block|nameid=magma|foot=1}}
– Sealbudsman talk/contr 16:11, 27 June 2016 (UTC)
Oh, I see, the planks show up correctly on Data values/Block IDs, it's just the magma block that needs a fix, now I know how to fix it. I didn't notice that when editing the page. nyuszika7h (talk) 16:13, 27 June 2016 (UTC)
