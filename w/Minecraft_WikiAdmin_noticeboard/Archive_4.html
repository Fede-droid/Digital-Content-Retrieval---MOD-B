Posting links is snafu'd

Unlogged in users have to preview the page before they can save an edit with links in it; but trying to save requires a captcha to be put in (things like 24-8=? or 3+29=?). The process goes: Click edit page, Put your text in, Be forced to click show preview, Get ability to save and try to do so, Be forced to enter chaptcha but can't click save a second time because it's disabled (i.e. forced to click preview again).

Captcha isn't on preview page but editors are required to be on preview page to save. Captcha requires save to work. So can't save and can't enter captcha. This is especially a problem because it also applies to talk/discussion pages. 75.164.152.61 00:24, 12 September 2011 (UTC)Reply[reply]

Moving pages

Just a heads-up to everyone who follows this page, pages should not be moved by copy-pasting them into the new title, they should be moved by using the "Move" link in the dropdown menu immediately to the left of the search box. Pages which have been moved via copy-paste must be history merged to restore their history; this is something that can only be done by administrators. If an admin needs help or is unable or unwilling to do so for whatever reason, post here or on my talk page and I'll help out. 「User:Dinoguy1000Special:Contributions/Dinoguy1000」? · ☎ Dinoguy1000 01:39, 14 September 2011 (UTC)Reply[reply]
