15 years of MCW brainstorming

On June 17, 2024, it would be exactly 15 years ever since the establishment of Minecraft Wiki. Looking back, I haven't seen many celebrating MCW's birthday. However, that shouldn't be the reason not to celebrate this one.

Because of that, we should brainstorm and decide what do we want to do for our birthday.

Announcement on our social media Edit

In all of our 15-year-old existence, we've never made any serious attempt to announce our wiki milestones. Therefore, the first step would be to actually announce the fact that we're now 15 years old. We can use wiki's site notice, Twitter, Mastedon and MCW Discord, where we could go with a rare @everyone ping too. We can accompany the announcement with a link to some sort of "more information" page. --TreeIsLife (talk) 16:13, 16 March 2024 (UTC)Reply[reply]

Special 15th Birthday Theme Edit

On June 17 2024, just for a single day, we can have a special, never seen before minecraft wiki theme for its 15th Birthday (Mojang is also changing minecraft.net theme to a special 15 year birthday theme). We can have gifs around the page of party poppers, few balloons going up when you enter the home page, a special birthday-themed logo, birthday-themed search bar and so much more!
