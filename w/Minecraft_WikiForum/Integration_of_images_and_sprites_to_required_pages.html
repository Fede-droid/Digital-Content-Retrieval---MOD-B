Integration of images and sprites to required pages

Proposal Edit

Why is the integration of images and sprites to required pages helpful?

Many pages in this wiki regarding blocks, structures, updates etc. have either very few images and/or sprites or no images and/or sprites. Images and sprites not only enhance the looks, but also give you quick information about how the mob/structure/item/block etc. looks like. Adding images and sprites can also increase reader’s visit duration as the page would actually look good with many images and sprites as references and not just a page filled with plain text.

How would this be implemented?

There are a lot of pages that need images and sprites which can be a tedious task for a single editor but can be done in a group of editors as a project. I propose Project IIS: Integration of images and sprites to required pages. It would be a community-open project which means that anybody can participate and help with the project.

If you are still confused as to where the images and sprites are to be added, refer to Nether Update where I have added images and sprites before their respective references.
