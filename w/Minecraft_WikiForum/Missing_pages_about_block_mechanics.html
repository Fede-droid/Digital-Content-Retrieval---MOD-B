Missing pages about block mechanics

The following discussion is closed. Please do not modify it.

We have hitbox#Block hitboxes and Block update. - Harristic / Talk 12:50, 18 May 2024 (UTC)

I have noticed that the wiki is missing some pages related to blocks:

  • Block hitboxes
  • Block placing

These 2 pages are closely related, so maybe they could be merged into just 1 page. However, there are many instances where the game places blocks, which are referred to as post-generation.

I think these 2 pages would benefit the wiki a lot, but getting all of the block hit-boxes would require a lot of effort from the wiki's editors. --Simanelix (T|C) 04:10, 3 April 2024 (UTC)

We have breaking, but for some reason we don't document placing anywhere on the wiki. There's a lot to go into, from the game placing blocks to the player's placing mechanics (bedrock bridging and all that). I definitely want to see this documented. plighting_engineerd (talk) 15:42, 3 April 2024 (UTC)
We have hitbox#Block hitboxes, but still missing some information. And Block update, for what happened when a block changing in Java Edition.--Chixvv (talk) 13:29, 6 April 2024 (UTC)
