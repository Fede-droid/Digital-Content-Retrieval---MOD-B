Redoing natural equipment on the Dungeons wiki

The following discussion is closed. Please do not modify it.

This has been implemented. - Harristic / Talk 19:23, 19 May 2024 (UTC)

Problem Edit

Throughout the MCD Wiki, on mob infoboxes, we include something called "natural equipment." This is essentially an item a mob has equipped. For a MCD:Vindicator, this is a MCD:Axe. But technically speaking, this is inaccurate - when looking at the game assets, vindicators don't have axes equipped at all. All the vindicator does is use the axe's model and texture - in essence, natural equipment is non-existent.

At the same time, there is merit in listing such a thing. It makes sense to write that vindicators have axes equipped because I mean, it looks like they do. Yet, it is an inaccuracy nonetheless, and can be misleading. I'd bet anyone reading this wasn't even aware that Minecraft Dungeons mobs don't have natural equipment.

Furthermore, it becomes a greater issue when considering unique equipment. Let's look at, say, MCD:Wither Skeletons - they have "stone swords", but these are just a reskinned sword, reusing the model but using a different texture. So how do we handle that? Well, if you look at the MCD:Mountaineer page, I attempted to solve this. The mountaineer axes they wield are simply axes with a unique texture.

Unfortunately, I hit another road block: MCD:Vindicator Chefs. Unlike other forms of unique equipment, the ladle they wield have entirely unique models. They still aren't able to be considered "real" equipment, but I can't exactly call them a reskin, either. So, how should we handle this?

Proposal Edit

I think one way to clear up some confusion would be to change the term "Natural equipment." Obviously, this term comes from the mainspace wiki, but it doesn't mean the same thing here - a Piglin actually does have "real" gold swords and crossbows, while a MCD:Piglin does not. So perhaps a term like "Equipped models" or something of the sort would be more accurate. Perhaps an explanation of this could be placed on the MCD:Mob page, so that readers can understand what it really means.

As for handling "unique" equipment, the problem is that it would be illogical to make a special page for them since they're simply models, but it would also be strange not to list them at all. Thus, I propose we don't include links. For a vindicator chef, it would simply say " Ladle" under "Equipped models" - a reader would understand by reading on the MCD:Mob page what an equipped model is, and thus know that a ladle is not a real item.

Discussion Edit
