Split pages like map and candle should have a Crafting section

Instead of putting dyed candle crafting just in § Obtaining, or duplicating the inforamtion into § Usage, the crafting recipes should be in their own section, § Crafting.

This proposal is for any Item or Block page with similar crafting recipes in § Obtaining and § Usage to use the § Crafting section instead. This proposal will mostly apply to pages that could be split but haven't been split. However, this change would not be limited to those pages.

This proposal should also apply to Map, even though it has no duplicate recipes. Map has recipes for explorer maps, which are obviously just another kind of map. It would make sense for the similar cloning recipes for explorer maps to be included with the normal map cloning recipes.

Finally, having a crafting section opens up the potential to have subsections in crafting that might make more sense than subsections in § Obtaining > § Crafting or § Usage > § Crafting

--Simanelix (T|C) 18:43, 3 March 2024 (UTC)Reply[reply]

 Oppose — “Obtaining” is meant to cover all ways of gaining an item. If we’re having “Crafting” as a first-class section, then “Obtaining” would need a rename to something like “Other methods of obtaining”, or all other methods of obtaining would need their own first-class sections. Both of those outcomes make little sense to me. I find no issue with “regular candles can be used to craft dyed candles” not being mentioned in “Usage”. — BabylonAS 18:52, 3 March 2024 (UTC)Reply[reply]
