Story Mode namespace custom content area background color

Since Minecraft Story Mode doesn't have namespace specific color for content area background as other namespaces - I come with a proposition of colors in two accent's to choose from:

purple light, purple dark

golden light, golden dark

Feel free to express your feedback and choice on the accent (golden/purple) we should go with ^^. Oakar567 (talk) 16:59, 6 May 2024 (UTC)Reply[reply]

 Support golden, I originally thought purple would look far better, but golden looks really gorgeous actually. It also fits the grass colour, and while it doesn't matter too much if the content background uses the dirt colour, consistency with the other themes would be nice. Thanks for making these! - Harristic / Talk 17:08, 6 May 2024 (UTC)Reply[reply]
I'd probably prefer purple because the dark golden background color seems a little unappealing, although I think the light purple might look better with some desaturation. –⁠Sonicwave talk 22:15, 6 May 2024 (UTC)Reply[reply]
