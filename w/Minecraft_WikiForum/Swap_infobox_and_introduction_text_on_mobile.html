Swap infobox and introduction text on mobile

Swap infobox and introduction text on mobile is a feature built into the MediaWiki's MobileFrontend extension, and it is enabled on Wikimedia wikis like Wikipedia. Infoboxes on the wiki are often very long, and it requires a long scroll to actually get an overview on what the page is talking about. By moving the introduction text up, it declutters the top page experience for mobile users and is a UX win. I imagine that is also somewhat important for improving retention rate when clicking from a search engine. Since the reader can find the page's topic faster, they will be less likely to be frustrated, think that it doesn't address the info they need and leave. This should be quite easy to implement. We just need to coordinate with Weird Gloop, test it out on dev wiki first, and then deploy it to production.
