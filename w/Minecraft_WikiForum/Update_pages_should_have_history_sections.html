Update pages should have history sections

This means pages like Tricky Trials should have a history section. i.e. Tricky Trials § History.

I noticed Revision 2552091 by BDJP007301, and realized that Tricky Trials does not have a history section. The reverted infomation would go in the history section, like this:

Here is an example of what some of the history table could look like. I'm not sure whether expanding this to include every change to the "Update 1.21" experimental toggle / data-pack, and every 1.21 snapshot and preview, would be a good idea, but I think a history section for some basic information would be good at least.
