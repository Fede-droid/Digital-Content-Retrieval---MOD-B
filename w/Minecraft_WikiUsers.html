Minecraft Wiki:Users

  • Language
  • Watch
  • View source

User refers to anybody who is logged into an account on the Minecraft Wiki. Unlike unregistered users (IP users), users can do the following:

  • Creating non-talk pages
  • Marking edits as minor
  • Moving pages
  • Gaining user rights
  • Uploading new files (but not overriding existing files)

Autoconfirmed users

Users, upon having been registered for 3 full days (72 hours) as well as making 5 edits, will automatically be added to the autoconfirmed user group. This group gives users more abilities, including the following:

  • Editing semi-protected pages
  • Moving pages without leaving a redirect
  • Moving files
  • Uploading new versions of existing files
  • Skipping CAPTCHA when adding external links to pages
  • Not be affected by IP-based rate limits
