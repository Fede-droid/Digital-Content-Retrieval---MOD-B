Minecraft x Youtooz

  • Language
  • Watch
  • Edit

Minecraft x Youtooz is an official collaboration between Minecraft and vinyl figure company Youtooz. The first of figures (Ender Dragon, Creeper, Wolf) happened on November 3, 2023,[1], and the second release (Warden, bees, Chickens, Minecraft Pin Set) happened on April 23, 2024.[2]

Shipping Edit

Minecraft Youtooz figures only ship to the following regions:[3]
