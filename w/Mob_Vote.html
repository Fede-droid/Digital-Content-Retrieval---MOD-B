Mob Vote

  • Language
  • Watch
  • Edit

The mob vote is a community poll that takes place during or just before Minecraft annual events. This allows viewers to vote for a new mob to be added to the game in a future update. Each mob receives its own video, introducing its behavior and impact on gameplay.

Starting from 2022, the vote takes place before the show goes live and players are able to cast their votes across three areas: a server on Bedrock Edition, via the Minecraft Launcher, and on minecraft.net. Starting from 2023, players can also vote in China Edition launcher. Previously, the vote was held on the official Minecraft page on Twitter during the livestream. The results of the mob votes are released during the livestream.

An unrelated prediction poll was also held in China, teasing the announcement at MINECON Earth 2018 of a Chinese "national treasure" coming to Minecraft. The panda won the poll, and was announced a few days later during the livestream.

There have been a few more minor mob votes, such as Jeb's llama vs alpaca vote, and the cat vote featuring Jellie, Zach and Coco as options for a new cat skin.
