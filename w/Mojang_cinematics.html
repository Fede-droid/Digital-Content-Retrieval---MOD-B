Update trailers Edit

Village and Pillage: Official Trailer Edit

Village and Pillage: Official Trailer (view on YouTube)
 
“

Join the party and discover Minecraft’s most adventurous update yet! Defend against new threats, build with new blocks and experience life in all-new villages.

 „

Summary Edit

The video opens on a shot of the sky, lowering to reveal a first person perspective of Alex on a path. She greets a wandering trader tending to two trader llamas, one a child, all approaching a village in a plains biome. A wolf instigates an encounter with the trader llama, it gets spat on. The scene cuts to a long shot of several villagers dancing throughout the village, singing a song made up of their distinctive hums while joined in the number by several farm animals. A nitwit shows off an iron sword at a smithing table, while another polishes a golden pickaxe with a grindstone, who Alex appears to wave at. A cat tries to take a cod from a fisherman standing beside the grindstone, before a cartographer peeks out of a window and throws several maps to Alex.

From there the scene cuts again to a group of several nitwits and nitwit children frolicking down a road, before getting chased by a pig ridden by a child near a building under construction with scaffolding, which a chicken falls off of. Two other children kick around a slime like a ball near a fountain. Soon, a raid occurs as many pillagers, vindicators, and a ravager emerge and there is much tension. A vindicator swings their axe at Alex, she deflects with a shield and incapacitates the illager with a diamond sword, which she then tosses at another vindicator. The ravager and its pillager rider leap into the air, sending many blocks, items, and chickens flying, before Alex immediately shoots the pillager with a crossbow, causing the ravager to run off to parts unknown. With that, Alex is declared hero of the village, and thus the surviving villagers celebrate with fireworks.

Buzzy Bees: Official Trailer Edit

Buzzy Bees: Official Trailer (view on YouTube)
 
“

Minecraft is buzzing in its latest update! Discover how bees breathe life into their surroundings, bringing speedier crop growth, homey hives, and sticky honey!

 „

Summary Edit

The video opens on a shot of three bees resting inside of a bee nest, waking up after being asleep for the night. They fly out to begin pollinating several flowers, and we get a first person perspective of one of the bees. It flies over a colony of rabbits, then flies down a colossal waterfall. Two bees eye a poppy, and playfully race to pollinate it first before the opposing bee knocks into the protagonist one. The bee falls onto the ground, shakes itself, dusts itself off, and then pollinates the flower anyway.

The video climaxes at a diagonal shot of several bees rushing to help out a farm, pollinating dozens if not hundreds of crops. The bees pass by an iron golem giving a poppy to children, fly over a store selling honeycombs, fly under a villager drinking a bottle of honey, sidestep many more mobs, before knocking into a pillager. The collision catches the illager off guard, causing them to fire their crossbow at a witch, exposing them for trying to steal honey. Several angry bees emerge from the beehive to confront the illagers, and they run away horrified into the wheat fields. A job well done, the bees all return to their homes and fall asleep again.

Nether Update: Official Trailer Edit

Nether Update: Official Trailer (view on YouTube)
 
“

Are you ready to get to know the darker side of Minecraft? On June 23 the Nether Update brings the heat to an already fiery dimension on Xbox One, PlayStation 4, Nintendo Switch, iOS, Android, Windows 10, and more. The update will also be available Java, and then rolls out on Windows, Mac OS, and Linux within 24 hours.

 „

Summary Edit

Alex and Steve happen upon a ruined portal and decide to fix it up. On the other side, they're introduced to the many biomes and creatures of the Nether, such as an enderman lurking in the warped forest. Soon, they end up ambushed by a squad of piglins, who shoot them dead with arrows. The two return, now wearing iron armor, and manage to survive long enough to retreat into a crimson forest, as the piglins ambush them from inside the fungi. This time, the heroes came prepared and place down a respawn anchor, only to end up dying and thus losing their armor.

From there, Steve tries to make peace with the piglins by gifting one rotten flesh. He gets killed. He gifts the same piglina gold ingot while wearing gold armor. He gets the piglins' respect. Steve and Alex then proceed to mine Nether gold ore, but are spotted by a ghast. After respawning they notice a hoglin and hunt it for meat. Following a hefty meal, they forge new netherite armor and fight countless mobs on top of a dune in a soulsand valley.

Caves & Cliffs Update: Part II – Official Trailer Edit

Caves & Cliffs Update: Part II - Official Trailer (view on YouTube)
 
“

Scale bigger mountains, unearth elaborate caves, and mine large ore veins in Part II of the Caves & Cliffs Update! Discover lush cave and dripstone cave biomes, increased world height, and updated terrain generation. Light up your world with candles to show what a savvy spelunker and master mountaineer you are!

 „

Summary Edit

Steve and Alex are exploring a mountain cave, with Steve feeling intimidated by a cobweb. After cowering behind Alex, she pulls out a torch, and soon they begin mining for copper. Feeling mischievous, Alex makes a shadow puppet of a spider using a dead bush, to Steve's horror. After some facial banter, Alex decides to leave on a minecart, but falls down a long track through a mineshaft into a lush cave. A spring catches her fall, meanwhile Steve continues mining and ends up falling into a dripstone cave himself, suffering great injuries through slapstick.

Meanwhile, Alex dances on big dripleaves. She then stumbles upon an amethyst geode. Steve is not so fortunate, getting burned and stabbed many times while trying to find his way out, as several real spiders apparently sit back and laugh at him. Alex befriends an axolotl, Steve tries to run in fear from a massive clutter of spiders. He falls down a hole and falls right on top of Alex, sending her down a waterfall into a cave full of monsters. In a panic, they use the materials they gathered over their journey to craft a spyglass, eyeing out an exit for them. With the axolotl bucketed, they scurry out of the cave and seal it with gravel, only to realize they are now at the top of the mountain.

To make matters worse, Steve is rammed off the cliff by a goat.

Sunny and Efe are exploring the underground with caution, when suddenly a chicken appears. Sunny tries to approach the chicken, only for it to run away. The two chase it through a mineshaft, briefly bumping into Steve and Alex, both confused by the encounter. Eventually, they run straight into the Deep Dark, setting off various sculk shriekers before losing the chicken in the ancient city. Engulfed by the shadow of the Warden, Sunny and Efe are terrified but remain calm as it tries to sniff them out. Sure enough, the chicken lays an egg, presumably out of fear, alerting it of their presence. The video ends on a cliffhanger.

Introducing New Default Skins! Edit

Introducing New Default Skins! (view on YouTube)
 
“

Minecraft is getting seven new default skins – meet Makena, Efe, Noor, Kai, Ari, Sunny, and Zuri! Just like Steve and Alex, the new default skins will help you tell your Minecraft stories whether you are exploring the Overworld, heading into an SMP adventure, or creating the build of your dreams! The new default skins will be available when you launch Minecraft from November 29.

 „

Summary Edit

Alex is building with wool on top of an oversized trader llama statue, before looking down at Steve and waving. Steve starts placing scaffolding to reach her and pass a redstone torch, but trips and falls off. Luckily, Ari arrives wearing elytra and grabs it, flying past a flock of parrots before dropping the torch to Zuri, who is mining resources with Sunny. Zuri runs off to return the torch, but is caught off guard by an emerging creeper, the two running over a wheat farm being tended to by Kai and Makena. Zuri then trips over a cat, dropping the torch once again.

The torch is caught by none other than Noor, who is mining for coal with Ari. Noor then falls into a minecart, and is propelled into the air by an extensive railway while another cat chases potentially the same creeper through a savanna. The torch next lands in the hands of Sunny, who is attempting to fish on the border between biomes. Sunny is momentarily confused by the torch, before getting hit and sunk by Noor's body. From there the torch sinks and is found by Efe, who is fighting a horde of drowned. Efe is also momentarily confused, but quickly manages to swim away and scurries onto the surface face first, before joining the chase with the cat and creeper.

Efe begins riding a pig, before bumping into Makena gliding. Efe hands the torch to Makena, who then finally returns it to Alex. From there, all nine of the heroes appear at the base of the llama statue and light up redstone lamps built into it, using their own torches. With that, Alex places the inciting toch onto the statue, activating a unique dispenser mechanism designed to resemble spitting, and everyone celebrates, but not before the creeper returns and nearly blows everyone up. The video ends on a shot of the full group in a light blue void, with the caption "tell your story, with default skins."

Minecraft Live 2021: Announcement Trailer (view on YouTube)
 
“

The trailer begins with a booming announcer with a 1980s-adjacent vocal filter, describing Minecraft Live as "pushing the boundaries of what was thought to be possible." "Four times more unpredictable," when a piglin is caught dancing, to its own embarrassment. Minecraft is turning 15 | Come celebrate with us! Edit

Minecraft is turning 15 | Come celebrate with us! (view on YouTube)
 
“

Minecraft will soon be 15 years old! To mark the occasion, we’re hosting a 15-day celebration packed with gifts for you, kicking off from May 15!

„

Summary Edit

In a void of green, neon blocks, a 15 appears. As it does, a large assortment of mobs, from bees to Noor, as an announcer narrates that Minecraft is turning 15 in 2024. As an iron golem lights several fireworks for baby villagers and an allay, the announcer elaborates, saying that there are "presents" as a sheep cartwheels quite impressively. Describing it as a "blocktacular celebration," Minecraft is going to announce fifteen things across fifteen days to celebrate the fifteenth anniversary of the game Minecraft. As this is explained, many things happen, such as a goat leaping onto a button that releases a flock of parrots, revealing a crafting cap, and a warden does what appears to be a dance with a sniffer.

After a cat awkwardly violates a creeper's personal space, Alex dances around a Nether Portal, tossing around gold nuggets as a piglin emerges, its interest piqued. An iron golem invites the camera into a hallway protected by an iron door, where a spyglass is placed on the ground. Using it, it is revealed that, on the opposite end of the hallway, is a pillager also using a spyglass, who runs away in fear upon being spotted. As the camera recedes down another path, a statue resembling Steve is shown, and looking away. The announcer promises "weird stuff," seguing into a room of skeletons dancing, who get disturbed by the intrusion and almost shoot the camera. The announcer and camera then move on to a picture of Jens looking at a hanging sign in a boat with a chest, which the announcer clarifies we are not meant to see and blurs.

The scene transitions into a study area, where a hoglin is resting, surrounded by various redstone blocks crafting cakes. A chest opens up, briefly revealing a mysterious symbol resembling a percentage, before the camera is quickly ejected from the room and cut off by a goat closing the door. That same goat proceeds to break many of the creeper-like blocks encasing everyone, creating a tunnel that leads to an enderman. An arrow is shot as a decorated pot, as a bat flies over to some villagers and an armored wolf around a campfire. The announcer promises that "it will be a blast," causing a frog to emerge and swallow the camera whole. Inside of the frog, it is revealed there is a kelp forest, with many tropical fish, glow squids, and a pufferfish.

The camera zooms out again, revealing Alex was reading a popup book of the previous scenes on a lectern. As she flips through various pages, a creeper emerges from inside the book, causing her to close the book and throw it away in a panic, right before it explodes. As the announcer announces "the ultimate Minecraft party and you're invited," many other mobs appear, including Steve, Efe, and Sunny. Zuri swoops in with elytra, as the camera tunnels through another set of blocks as more and more mobs appear, before revealing the logo of the anniversary.

Minecraft Celebrates the Community! (Yes, that means you!) Edit

Minecraft Celebrates the Community! (Yes, that means you!) (view on YouTube)
 
“

The Minecraft community have generated so much content on YouTube, that when the trillionth click was clacked this video appeared. Welcome to a world that the community inspired, with their seemingly endless creativity and love for a little game about blocks. A trillion views means A LOT of content, so we couldn’t fit it all in there but can you spot all the references in our #Minecraftmuseum? Be warned, you might have to watch it a trillion times to catch them all.

 „

Summary Edit

The video opens in "Minecraft build 0.0.11a, 2009" as a classic player builds with dirt blocks. A time traveler emerges from the ground with a shovel, asking "which build is this?" The classic player simply says "one of the first," causing the time traveler to take them down into a hole and sky dive into "Minecraft build 1.1???, present day." The classic player asks if the strange land they encountered is a DLC, only to be surprised when they find out it's actually built by Minecraft YouTubers, which have now generated a trillion views. The classic player is then revealed to be so old that they don't know what mining is, asking why people are digging a big hole.

The Classic Player continues, asking where people learn their skills, remarking they couldn't find a tutorial. The time traveler simply replies there "is no tutorial for this level of creativity." They walk past a redstone contraption and both remnark that it's dangerous. A sea of arrows fall from the sky, forcing them to take cover, only to be affected by an antigravity mod. They then pass a Nether Portal under construction, which the time traveler refers to as being built by a hack. The two then uncover a griefer burning down a house, which the classic player is horrified by and asks for the legality of. The classic player asks why to do all of this, and the time traveler explains the monetary incentives of being a YouTuber.

After arriving at a beach and bumping into a disappearing horse, the classic player asks if "this was all part of Mojang's plan." They are told no in a condescending manner, before another time traveler emerges from the sand to propose showing the two the future.

Trivia Edit

  • Although they were not formally introduced until Minecraft Live 2022, Sunny, Noor, and Efe made several appearances in trailers for the Wild Update.
  • In several trailers, Steve's beard is treated as a smile, emoting in various ways.
  • Curiously, several of the unused green axolotl can be seen during the Caves & Cliffs Update: Part I – Official Trailer, suggesting they were scrapped late into the production of the video.

Errors Edit

  • During Village and Pillage: Official Trailer, a piece of redstone dust erroneously colored blue could be seen as a reward given out by Hero of the Village. This led to rumors of it being a teaser for the following update, with some speculating it to be cobalt and the update being called "Combat and Cobalt."
  • In the Armored Paws drop - Official Trailer, the armadillo featured is missing its body.
  • During Minecraft is turning 15 | Come celebrate with us! a pillager can be seen with its in-game texture, as opposed to the minimalist style featured in the rest of the video.
