Peter Hont is the interim audio director[1] for Mojang Studios who helped compose the music for Minecraft Dungeons, both for the Mainland levels and all current DLC levels.[2] He is most known for his Minecraft Dungeons: Original Game Soundtrack album.

Official Minecraft Dungeons soundtracks Edit

  • Minecraft Dungeons (Original Game Soundtrack) (June 24, 2020)
  • Minecraft Dungeons: Jungle Awakens (Original Game Soundtrack) (June 24, 2020)
  • Minecraft Dungeons: Creeping Winter (Original Game Soundtrack) (November 5, 2020)
  • Minecraft Dungeons: Howling Peaks (Original Game Soundtrack) (December 1, 2020)
  • Minecraft Dungeons: Flames of the Nether (Original Game Soundtrack) (February 19, 2021)
  • Minecraft Dungeons: Hidden Depths (Original Game Soundtrack) (May 25, 2021)
  • Minecraft Dungeons: Echoing Void (Original Game Soundtrack) (July 25, 2021)
  • Minecraft Dungeons: Ultimate Additions (Original Game Soundtrack) (October 20, 2021)
  • Minecraft Dungeons: Seasonal Adventures (Original Game Sountrack) (June 5, 2023)
