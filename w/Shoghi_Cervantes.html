Shoghi Cervantes is a member of Mojang Studios. Before working on Minecraft, they[4] worked on (and are the founder of) PocketMine, which is a server software for Bedrock Edition.

They currently work for the team of development for Bedrock Edition and Minecraft Dungeons.[7]

Trivia Edit

  • They are red-green colorblind.[8][9]
  • A splash in Pocket Edition is titled "Blame shogchips!", referencing a joke site which allowed users to 'blame' Shoghi.[10]
