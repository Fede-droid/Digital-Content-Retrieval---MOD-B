Two tables, identical? Edit

Latest comment: 11 October 20213 comments2 people in discussion

The two tables in the Natural Generation section appear identical. Is there a reason they are separate tables? SirDaddicus (talk) 18:24, 23 September 2021 (UTC)Reply[reply]

Template:LootChestItem always displays Java and Bedrock tables of data. Sometimes they are different, but often they are the same. As there has been an effort underway to make Java and Bedrock be more equal internally, the Lua module that fills in the template will eventually have to be fixed to display one table, because eventually they may be the same almost always.
