Where you will find all the textures Edit

All default textures are in .minecraft/versions/1.x(e.g. 1.19)/1.x.jar(open e.g. with 7zip)/asset/minecraft/textures

Missing Progression Texture Ages Edit

Stem textures do not have their ages specified, they have unique textures for ages 0 through 7 Hanging Mangrove Propagule textures also do not have their ages specified, they have unique textures for ages 0 through 4
