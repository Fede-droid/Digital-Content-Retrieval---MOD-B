Forcing linebreaks in an otherwise variable-width element in a variable-width skin is just asking for trouble, since it is literally impossible to account for every possible screen resolution - anyone with a resolution wider than about 1024px, for instance, is going to end up with lots of wasted space on either side of the box, which will be unnecessarily tall.
