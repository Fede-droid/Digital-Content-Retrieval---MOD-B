The Wild Update is a major update released on June 7, 2022, as Java Edition 1.19 and Bedrock Edition 1.19.0,[1] themed around "scary things" and natural wilderness. It introduces the deep dark and mangrove swamp biomes; ancient cities; mobs such as the allay, the frog, the tadpole and the warden, as well as new items obtainable only in these new biomes.

On Java Edition, the first publicly-available build containing The Wild Update content is Deep Dark Experimental Snapshot 1, which was released on February 17, 2022. The first pre-release for The Wild Update was released on May 18, 2022 and the first release candidate was released on June 2, 2022.

Notable features Edit

Main articles: Java Edition 1.19, Bedrock Edition 1.19.0 and Education Edition 1.19.52
New blocks
  • Froglight
    • Obtained from frogs eating small magma cubes.
    • Froglight color depends on the frog type.
  • Frogspawn
    • Spawned in the water after breeding two frogs.
    • Hatches into tadpoles.
    • Cannot be obtained even with Silk Touch.
  • Reinforced Deepslate
    • Found in the centerpiece of the ancient city.
    • Cannot be moved with a piston, and mining is slow and will not drop the item.
  • New mangrove wood type, found in the mangrove swamp biome:
    • Mangrove Propagule
    • Mangrove Leaves
    • Mangrove Log and its stripped variant
    • Mangrove Wood and its stripped variant
    • Mangrove Roots
    • Mangrove Planks
      • Mangrove Stairs
      • Mangrove Slab
      • Mangrove Sign
      • Mangrove Button
      • Mangrove Door
      • Mangrove Fence
      • Mangrove Fence Gate
      • Mangrove Pressure Plate
      • Mangrove Trapdoor
  • New mud blocks:
    • Mud
      • Found in mangrove swamps.
      • Can also be created by using a water bottle on dirt.
      • Converts to clay when placed above a pointed dripstone.
    • Packed Mud
      • Crafted using a mud and a wheat.
      • Mud Bricks
        • Mud Brick Stairs
        • Mud Brick Slab
        • Mud Brick Wall
    • Muddy Mangrove Roots
  • New sculk blocks, all found in the deep dark:
    • Sculk
    • Sculk Catalyst
      • Mobs killed near a sculk catalyst will cause nearby blocks to either be converted to sculk or have sculk veins grow.
      • Will occasionally spawn a sculk sensor or a inert sculk shrieker.
    • Sculk Shrieker
      • Causes a shriek sound and particle effect to be made when activated by the player or nearby sculk sensors
      • Has a warning system where the player can only activate the sculk shrieker three times; upon the fourth shriek, a warden will spawn.
      • Sculk shrieker placed by the player or generated by sculk catalyst cannot spawn the warden.
    • Sculk Sensor
      • Detects any vibrations within a 8-block radius.
      • Vibration detection can be dampened with wool or carpet.
      • Emits a redstone signal depending on vibration frequency and close proximity.
    • Sculk Vein
New items
  • Bucket of Tadpole
    • Obtained by using a water bucket on a tadpole.
    • Can be used to transport the tadpole to a new location.
  • Echo Shard
    • Obtained from ancient city chests.
    • Used to craft a recovery compass.
  • Goat Horns
    • Obtained from goats ramming into blocks native to their biome.
    • Has eight different variants, four from normal goats, and four from screaming goats.
  • Recovery Compass
    • Used to point to the location of the player's last death.
  • New music disc:
    • Samuel Åberg - 5
      • Obtained by crafting with 9 disc fragments.
  • Spawn eggs:
    • Allay Spawn Egg
    • Frog Spawn Egg
    • Tadpole Spawn Egg
    • Warden Spawn Egg
    • Ender Dragon Spawn Egg
    • Iron Golem Spawn Egg
    • Snow Golem Spawn Egg
    • Trader Llama Spawn Egg‌[Bedrock Edition only]
    • Wither Spawn Egg
New mobs
  • Allay
    • Found in pillager outpost cages or woodland mansion jail cells.
    • Follows the player around when holding an item.
    • Will collect any item close by and drop it to either an activated note block or the player.
  • Frog
    • Spawns in swamps and mangrove swamps.
    • Eats small slimes and magma cubes, the latter produces froglights.
    • Can be bred using slime balls.
  • Tadpole
    • Hatches from frogspawn
    • Will mature to a frog; its type depending on the biome they are matured in.
    • Can be moved with a water bucket.
  • Warden
    • Powerful, yet evadable hostile mob, with the most health out of any mob.
    • Spawns in from above after enough sculk shriekers have been activated.
    • A blind mob that can only detect players and mobs using vibration, smell, and touch.
    • Upon finding a target, the warden roars and pursues the target.
    • If it cannot reach the target, the warden does a sonic boom attack that bypasses armor, enchantments, shields, and other mob-related armor.
    • If not aggravated for 60 seconds, the warden will burrow down and despawn.
    • Drops a sculk catalyst when killed.
New non-mob entities
  • Boat with Chest
    • A variant of the boat used to also provide item storage.
  • Mangrove Boat, including a chest variant
New biomes
  • Deep Dark
    • Found deep underground, commonly under mountainous areas.
    • Generates with sculk, sculk veins, sculk catalysts, sculk sensors, and sculk shriekers.
  • Mangrove Swamp
    • Generates with mangrove trees, mud, dead bush, and lily pads.
New structures
  • Ancient city[2]
    • An underground structure found in the deep dark.
    • Has a higher chance of finding enchanted golden apples than any other structures prior.
    • Three music discs, disc fragments, echo shards, and a enchanted book with a random level of Swift Sneak can be found here.
  • Mangrove tree
New gameplay
  • New enchantment:
    • Swift Sneak
      • Used on leggings to provide a quicker sneaking speed.
  • New effect:
    • Darkness
      • Occurs upon activating a sculk shrieker or being in close proximity with a warden.
      • Similar to blindness, but with a pulsating darkness limiting visibility with 14 blocks.
New soundtrack
  • Four new ambient tracks from Lena Raine were added; all but "Ancestry" would play on the main menu until Trails & Tales.
    • "Ancestry" (from Minecraft: Caves & Cliffs (Original Game Soundtrack))
      • Plays in the deep dark.
    • "Aerie", "Firebugs", & "Labyrinthine"
      • Plays in forests, birch forests, dark forests, old growth taigas jungles, lush caves, swamps, and mangrove swamps.

Trivia Edit

  • While this update introduces mud as a block, mud has existed in the franchise as a fluid in Minecraft Earth. The properties of mud in this update are identical to a block.
  • Fireflies were initially planned for this update, but were ultimately scrapped as some species of fireflies are poisonous to some species of frogs in real life.[3] This decision was heavily criticized by the community, despite the community being responsible for its cancellation.
  • To promote the wild update, Mojang released a free choose your own adventure game on minecraft.net called Craft Your Own Adventure: Wild Tales.[4]
  • Kingbdogz stated in a tweet that the souls in the warden's chest is incredibly important to its lore.[5]
  • The official artwork and trailer for the update is notable for marking the first appearance of Sunny and Noor, before their official introduction into the game.
  • The community constantly referred to this update as the “Mild Update” because of some scrapped features such as the firefly and the birch forest overhaul.
