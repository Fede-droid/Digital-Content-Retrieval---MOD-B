Tricky Trials is an upcoming major update set to release in mid-2024 as Java Edition 1.21 and Bedrock Edition 1.21.0.[1] The update focuses on the trial chambers, which serves as a place for "combat adventures" and features related to "tinkering".[2] The update adds the crafter, 2 new hostile mobs, the trial chambers, and many other features. In the trial chambers, players can find:

  • The breeze, a tricky mob that can sweep players off their feet.[3]
  • Trial spawners and vaults, which reward players for completing trials.
  • Ominous bottles, which can be used to trigger ominous trials for even bigger rewards, including the mace.
  • The mace, a new melee weapon that can be crafted using a heavy core and a breeze rod.
    • Deals 6 damage, identical to that of a iron sword in Java Edition and a stone sword in Bedrock Edition.
    • Has an attack speed of 0.6, 40% slower than that of a golden, diamond, or netherite axe.
    • Attacking after having fallen at least 1.5 blocks will perform a smash attack. The more blocks a player has fallen, the greater the damage of the smash attack.
      • Successfully striking a target with a smash attack will negate any damage accumulated from the fall, similar to how a wind charge works, as well as reseting all vertical momentum.
      • Maces have a unique explosive particle effect when executing a smash attack.
      • In addition to the normal unique smash attack sound, a different unique sound is played performing a smash attack from a fall of more than 5 blocks.
      • Other entities near a smash-attacked target will receive knockback, with a stronger effect when having fallen more than 5 blocks before attacking.
      • Smash attacks will activate the Wind Burst enchantment.
    • Can be repaired with breeze rods using an anvil.
    • Has three exclusive enchantments: Density, Breach, and Wind Burst.
    • Players can use a mace in combination with wind charges to launch up and deliver devastating smash attacks on their enemies.

The features for the Tricky Trials update were under an experimental toggle, called "Update 1.21". The toggle was used to test 1.21 features before the update is released. In versions where it exists, the toggle must be enabled in order to access the 1.21 features. Java Edition 1.20.6 and Bedrock Edition 1.20.81 were the last versions to have these toggles, and after them, as 1.21 snapshots and previews released, the toggle was removed.

Main articles: Java Edition 1.21 and Bedrock Edition 1.21.0
New blocks
  • Crafter
    • Crafted using five iron ingots, two redstone dusts, a crafting table, and a dropper.
    • Used to craft an item when powered on.
  • Heavy Core
    • Obtained from unlocking ominous vaults.
    • Used to craft the mace.
  • Trial Spawner
    • Generates in trial chambers.
    • Spawns a limited number of mobs.
    • Once all the mobs are defeated, the trial spawner will eject a reward.
  • Ominous Trial Spawner
    • Changes from a trial spawner should a player approach it with Trial Omen.
    • Spawn mobs with equipment, as well as shooting potions and projectiles.
    • Ejects a different set of loot compared to the normal trial spawner.
  • Vault
    • Generates in trial chambers.
    • Unlocks with a trial key and will eject loot once per player.
  • Ominous Vault
    • Generates in trial chambers.
    • Has a unique loot table compared to the normal vault.
  • New copper blocks:
    • Chiseled Copper
    • Copper Grate
      • Can allow light to shine through.
    • Copper Door
    • Copper Trapdoor
    • Copper Bulb
      • Light level depends on oxidation state.
      • Can be toggled on with redstone.
      • Does not need power to continue to emit light.
  • New tuff blocks:
    • Tuff Stairs
    • Tuff Slab
    • Tuff Wall
    • Chiseled Tuff
    • Polished Tuff
      • Polished Tuff Stairs

      • Tuff Brick Stairs

      • Chiseled Tuff Bricks
New items
  • Breeze Rod
    • Obtained as drops from breezes.
    • Can be used to craft wind charge, mace, and flow armor trim.
    • Can also be used to brew potion of wind charged.
  • Wind Charge
    • Used as a projectile to knock entities back and activate certain redstone components.
    • Used by the breeze.
    • Can also be crafted with a breeze rod.
  • Mace
    • New melee weapon.
    • Has a powerful smash attack that can be performed by attacking with the mace while falling, and deals more damage the farther the player falls.
    • Has 3 exclusive enchantments:
      • Density: Increases the damage of the smash attack per block fallen before the smash attack is performed.
      • Breach: Reduces the effectiveness of armor protecting from damage dealt by the mace.
      • Wind Burst: Creates a small wind burst when the smash attack is performed, similar to the one generated by a wind charge
    • Crafted using a heavy core on top of a breeze rod.
  • Ominous Bottle
    • Obtained from unlocking vaults.
    • Can also be obtained as drops by raid captains.
  • Trial Explorer Map
    • Used to find a trial chamber.
  • Trial Key
    • Obtained from trial spawner.
    • Used to unlock a vault.
  • Ominous Trial Key
    • Obtained from ominous trial spawner.
    • Used to unlock an ominous vault.
  • New potions:
    • Potion of Infestation
      • Brewed by using a stone.
    • Potion of Oozing
      • Brewed by using a slime block.
    • Potion of Weaving
      • Brewed by using a cobweb.
    • Potion of Wind Charging
      • Brewed by using a breeze rod.
  • New tipped arrows:
    • Arrow of Infestation
    • Arrow of Oozing
    • Arrow of Weaving
    • Arrow of Wind Charging
  • New banner patterns:

      • Obtained from unlocking ominous vaults.

      • Obtained from unlocking vaults.
  • New pottery sherds:
    • Flow Pottery Sherd
    • Guster Pottery Sherd
    • Scrape Pottery Sherd
  • New armor trims:
    • Bolt Armor Trim
      • Obtained from unlocking vaults.
    • Flow Armor Trim
      • Obtained from unlocking ominous vaults.
  • New music discs:
    • Aaron Cherof - Precipice
      • Obtained from unlocking vaults.
    • Lena Raine - Creator
      • Obtained from unlocking ominous vaults.
    • Lena Raine - Creator (Music Box)
      • Obtained from breaking decorated pots in trial chambers.

New mobs
  • Bogged
    • Spawns in swamps and mangrove swamps.
    • Can also be found in trial chambers.
    • Shoots tipped arrows of poison.
  • Breeze
    • Spawns in trial chambers.
    • Jumps around and fires wind charges to deal knockback.
    • Drops breeze rods when killed.
New structures
  • Trial Chambers
    • Giant underground structures made of tuff and copper blocks that provide players a mid-game combat challenge.
    • The only place where breezes, trial spawners, and vaults show up.
    • Consists of several chambers connected together through corridors.
    • Each chamber has a trial spawner configured to either spawn the breeze, a melee mob (zombie, husk, or spider), a small melee mob (silverfish, baby zombie, cave spider, or slime), or a ranged mob (stray, skeleton, bogged).
New gameplay
  • Ominous events
    • Can be activated with Bad Omen.
    • When near a trial chamber, it starts an ominous trial.
    • When near a village, it starts a raid.
  • New enchantments for the mace:
    • Breach
      • Reduces the effectiveness of armor.
    • Density
      • Increases damage dealt per block fallen.
    • Wind Burst
      • Creates a wind charged effect upon executing a smash attack.
      • Cannot be obtained from the enchanting table.
  • New effects:
    • Infested
      • Entities have a 10% chance upon being hurt to spawn 1-3 silverfish.
    • Oozing
      • Upon death, entities can spawn two slimes within a 5x5x5 area.
    • Raid Omen
      • Occurs when an entity is near a valid village.
      • Causes a raid to start.
    • Trial Omen
      • Occurs when an entity is near a trial spawner.
      • Starts an ominous trial.
    • Weaving
      • Upon death, entities leave behind 2-3 cobwebs within a 7x7x7 area.
      • Entities with weaving can also move through cobwebs faster.
    • Wind Charged
      • Upon death, entities release a burst of wind that deals knockback.
