The Universal Event was an event server made in collaboration with Universal Pictures. There are six districts in the map, which are designed as a theme park. Players could complete two sets of quests to obtain two character creator items. The map contained 6 minigames and 3 parkour courses.

Tickets Edit

Tickets are a currency obtained by completing parkour, playing minigames, finding golden clappers, and completing achievements. They are used to purchase items from various shops around the server.

Golden Clappers Edit

There are 12 golden clappers in hidden places around the server. For finding all of them, the player is rewarded with the Clapperboard static back character creator item.

  • Screenshot of the Clapperboard back item

Districts Edit

The server is divided into 6 main districts: the entrance at the bottom of the map, Courthouse Square in the center, and Jaws Lake, Shrek's Swamp, Wall Street, and Western Street around it. Each district has a distinct theme as well as unique shops, parkour, and minigames. The quick travel menu can be used to view a map of the server as well as teleport to each area.

Entrance Edit

The entrance is the location at which players spawn. In the center is a replica of the rotating Universal globe fountain found at Universal Studios in real life. Behind the spawn point is the way out of the theme park, blocked by a gate. To the left is the entrance to Wall Street, to the right is the entrance to Western Street, and directly forward is Courthouse Square on the other side of a road.

Players in the 7x7 area around the spawn point are given Invisibility for 99,999 seconds. Players who move out of this area have the effect removed.

Trivia Edit

  • This is not only the first collaborative event server (the closest previous being a promotion for Minecraft Legends), but the first to be released to celebrate a holiday as opposed to a specific Minecraft release.
  • In one of the promotional images, a character resembling Zuri can be seen in the background, the only difference being noticeably different hair.
  • The Wall Street and Western Street cutouts appear to reuse spritework from the Trails & Tales Event; in particular, Efe and a camel.
  • Notably, the map correctly names the shark from Jaws as Bruce instead of attributing the title to him, as is common.
