Hi I'm Anachronist (former username Amatulic). I started out as an occasional visitor here to look up stuff with my young son who plays Minecraft with me. Somehow I ended up being a frequent contributor and eventually an administrator/bureaucrat on this Minecraft wiki.

My primary other online presence is as an administrator on the English Wikipedia. I also contribute sporadically to my mathematics-themed blog, in which I wrote a few articles related to D&D gaming and Minecraft.
