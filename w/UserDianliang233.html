Hi, I'm Dianliang233 (电量). I'm from Shanghai, China. I'm a Global interface maintainer, director, patroller on the Chinese wiki and an admin on the Literary Chinese wiki. I occasionally edit the English wiki as well and I help shitpost on the Wiki's Twitter account. Outside the Minecraft Wiki, I am also an admin on Fandom Chinese Community Central.

IRL I'm a native speaker of Chinese (simplified, specifically) and I speak English as well. I like writing code which I didn't start to learn until I joined here, and I've learned HTML/CSS/JavaScript/TypeScript and Python. I'm mostly a frontend developer and I love using frameworks like React and Vue, and Next.js and Nuxt.js. I'm currently working on interactive tools and calculators for the wiki.

If you want to contact me, feel free to leave a message on the talk page or on Discord (I'm dianliang).
