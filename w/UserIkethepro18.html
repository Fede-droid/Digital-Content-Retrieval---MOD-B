Versions of Minecraft I own Edit

At this point it's getting to be like Skyrim with the number of ways to play this damn game and it's spinoffs

Icon Version Copies Thoughts
Xbox One Edition 1 My first introduction to the game of Minecraft and ultimately one of the best versions to ever be released, only ulitmately beaten by the other more updated Legacy versions
Xbox 360 Edition 2 Once again a true classic surpassed only by the PS4 edition. I ended up with 2 copies because I bought 1 and then another came in a 360 games bundle I got. Can't complain
Java Edition 2 On the topic of good versions of Minecraft, we come to the classic way to play. It took me far too long to get Java edition, and now I have 2 copies. Nice
Bedrock Edition 4 Now onto less so good versions of the game. It's not bad, and there's a reason why this one has the most sales, but there are just better ways to play
"China Edition" 2 If we're going to talk weird ways to play Minecraft, this is one of them. I played this on my phone and PC for a bit (then they started asking for the government ID) so I think it's safe to say that this is basically it's own Minecraft spin off. It's quite far removed from the original game, and it's based on Bedrock Edition so it's a quirky mess
Raspberry Pi Edition 1 You probably haven't even heard of this. Which is good, because it is not very good. It's essentially a cut down fork of Pocket Edition Alpha v0.6.1 so there's nowt to do in it. You're supposed to do programming with it to make it do stuff but my brain can't handle that
Minecraft Legends 1 Taking a step away from the main Minecraft games, we get this wonderful little thing. Why they have to do my boy dirty like that. Still a bit peeved that they canned this so quickly, it was rather rude of them
Minecraft Dungeons 1 It's just good. I can't lie I'm a quiet fan of this game and if you've never played it before, you really should give it a go. It's arguably the most successful Minecraft spin off ever, even getting it's own spin off in the form of Minecraft Dungeons Arcade so it's clearly good
Minecraft: Story Mode 1 I got all 8 episodes and I've played around 0 of them. It's not a bad game, from what I've heard, but the point and click style of it just doesn't provide my brain with the constant stream of dopamine it needs to stay alive
Minecraft Earth 1/0 I got this game very briefly before it shut down, but I was never able to really actually play it because my crappy old phone didn't support AR. It may have been a good game but the timing was the worst possible and it died instantly. Can't help but feel bad for all of the devs that worked so hard on it
