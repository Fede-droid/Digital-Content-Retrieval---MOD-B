Hello, I am one of the admins on the wiki. My primarily focus is on Java Edition, though I also have the Bedrock Edition for Windows 10 as well as Minecraft Dungeons.

I also own a bot account (User:Sonicbot32), which can be used to quickly perform repetitive tasks without having them show in recent changes.

If you wish to contact me, feel free to leave a message on my talk page. I may also occasionally check the wiki's Discord server.
