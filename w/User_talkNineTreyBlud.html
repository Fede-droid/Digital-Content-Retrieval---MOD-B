Formatting of disambiguation pages Edit

Latest comment: 29 September 20202 comments2 people in discussion

I see that you have added icons and extra links to the descriptions of the entries of disambiguation pages, such as Golem. Icons are generally discouraged as they distract the reader and may cause loading problems, but they may come right after bullets, along with other exceptions.
