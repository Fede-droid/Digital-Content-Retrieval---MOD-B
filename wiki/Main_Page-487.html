Did you know…

From Wookieepedia's newest articles, expansions, and overhauls:

  • …that visitors could invoke the Rite of Hospitality to claim temporary lodging on the frozen planet of Thelj?
  • …that the Rebel Alliance operated flight schools on Farstey and Homon?
  • …that the Vandrayk Scale was a measurement that related likelihood for an asteroid to house the exogorth species?
  • …that Zabrak love ballads were a type of traditional music with over twenty verses?
  • …that David Tennant won a Daytime Emmy for voicing Huyang in Star Wars: The Clone Wars?
  • …that the design of the Nautolan Clariah was based on actress Anjelica Huston?

Good articles on Wookieepedia

A Good article is an article that adheres to certain quality standards but cannot reach Featured status due to its limited content. At present, there are 3,081 Good articles, out of a total of 191,275 articles on Wookieepedia. Articles that no longer meet the criteria can be proposed for improvement or removal at the Good article reviews page.

A small check mark () in the top right corner of an article's page indicates that the article has achieved Good status.

On this day…

May 30:

  • 1952 – Mike W. Barr is born.
  • 1973 – Chris Reiff is born.
  • 1979 – Star Wars Weekly 66 published.
  • 1984 – Return of the Jedi Weekly 50 published.
  • 2000 – Star Wars: Republic: Prelude to Rebellion trade paperback published.
  • 2001 – Star Wars (1998) 30 published.
  • 2002 – Star Wars Episode II: Attack of the Clones video game released.
  • 2002 – Star Wars: Rogue Squadron II: Rogue Leader: Prima's Official Strategy Guide published.
  • 2005 – Star Wars Insider 82 published.
  • 2006 – Legacy of the Force: Betrayal published.
  • 2007 – Knights of the Old Republic 17 published.
  • 2007 – Star Wars: Panel to Panel Volume 2: Expanding the Universe published.
  • 2012 – Blood Ties: Boba Fett is Dead 2 published.
  • 2012 – Star Wars: Knight Errant Volume 2: Deluge published.
  • 2013 – Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic released on iOS.
  • 2017 – The Rise of a Hero published.
  • 2018 – Lando - Double or Nothing 1 published.
  • 2019 – Solo: A Star Wars Story: Expanded Edition published in the UK.
  • 2023 – Star Wars Legends Epic Collection: The Original Marvel Years Vol. 6 published.
  • 2023 – John Beasley dies.
